## Video Attributes

- `muted` - Video starts muted
- `playsinline` - Video plays inline on mobile devices
- `loop` - Video loops continuously
- `controls` - Shows video controls (play, pause, volume, etc.)
- `width` and `height` - Dimensions of the video player
- `autoplay` - Video starts playing automatically (works best with muted)
